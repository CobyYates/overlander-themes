About This Run

THE TRIP

Four days across the historic Mojave Road, the same wagon route used to cross the desert in the 1860s. We'll start at Avi Casino on the Colorado, cross the Piute Range, camp two nights at classic dispersed sites, and finish at Afton Canyon. This is a moderate run — sand, washboard, some rocky sections, but nothing technical. Stock 4WD with all-terrains and a recovery kit can handle it; built rigs will have an easier time.

We move as a group, lead and sweep stay on the radio, and we're never separated by more than the next ridge. Bring four days of water minimum (1.5 gal/person/day), full fuel out of Avi, and your sense of humor — there will be sand stuck in places you don't expect for the rest of the year.
